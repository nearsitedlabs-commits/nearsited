FAQ

Asked & answered.

i.How is this different from running PageSpeed myself?
PageSpeed gives you one technical score. Nearsited gives you the lead, the full audit, the ranked issues, and a written pitch — for every business in a city — in under two minutes.
ii.Where do leads come from?
Google Places for discovery, then a layered AI audit on each business — performance, SEO, mobile, UX, trust. You can also import your own URL lists.
iii.Can I edit the AI pitch before sending?
Every pitch is editable. Adjust tone (direct / friendly / formal), length, and the angle it leans into. The AI gives you a 90% draft so you ship in minutes, not hours.
iv.What if a business has no website at all?
Nearsited classifies leads into five categories: no website, social-only, platform-only (Wix/Squarespace), has website, and weak website. Each gets a different pitch angle.
v.Do you do cold email sending?
No — we generate the pitch, you send it from your own tool (Gmail, Apollo, Instantly, whatever you use). Sending in-product is on the roadmap.
vi.Who built this?
Again Labs, a sub-studio inside an event-management agency. Built by people who know prospecting hurts and wanted to fix it for themselves first.
